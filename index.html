How each scenario works
01
Read the Scenario
Students encounter a real-world attack unfolding in real time through messages, calls, and events.
02
Break It Down
Each step of the attack is revealed one at a time. Students annotate their thinking at every stage.
03
Test Knowledge
A 5-question quiz checks comprehension with instant feedback and explanations.
04
Discuss
Guided questions prompt students to connect the scenario to their own lives and protect their families.
05
Export PDF
Every response, annotation, and quiz result exports as a clean PDF for grading or portfolios.
